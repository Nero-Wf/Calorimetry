To start the recording of real-time data of the Kalorimeter, insert the COM Port and press the "start" button.
To see the Data displayed, go to the second tab.
To stop the data recording, simply press the "stop" button. The data will be saved as an excel file called "Data", don't forget to rename it before starting an new run.

Calorimeter Use:
-Input operating points in the format (time, temperature, [flowrate 1, flowrate 2])
-Input substance data in the format ([weight A, weight B], [volume A, volume B], [molar mass A, molar mass B], [Substance assignement 1, Substance assignement 2])
-Input Strategy with operating points and substance data
